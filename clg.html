About St. George's College, Aruvithura

Established in 1965 and affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, St. George's College, Aruvithura is a prestigious institution committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. Nestled in a serene, green campus, our college provides an environment where knowledge and values go hand in hand.
