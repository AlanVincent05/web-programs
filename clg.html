St. George's College, Aruvithura

Established in 1965 and affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, St. George's College, Aruvithura is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering holistic development in a serene, green campus. Our mission is to shape students into responsible citizens through academic excellence and value-based learning.
